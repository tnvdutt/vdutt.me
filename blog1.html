My Journey as an Introverted Tech Enthusiast

Published on October 17, 2024

By Vishnu Dutt

For the longest time, I've identified as an introvert—someone who enjoys the quiet and comfort of staying behind the scenes. But as the world continues to become more connected, I’ve realized the value of sharing and connecting with others. So, here I am, stepping out of my comfort zone.

I’m passionate about technology, especially in areas like telecom, optical networks, and connectivity (if it has cables, waves, or signals, I’m in!). Writing this blog is my way of finding a voice and, maybe, a way to connect with fellow tech enthusiasts.

For an introvert like me, this blog is a way to open up and share my thoughts, knowledge, and experiences. It’s about stepping toward becoming the extroverted version of myself that I’ve always admired—one post at a time.
